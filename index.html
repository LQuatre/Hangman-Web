Modern Hangman Web

Hangman is a classic word game in which you must guess as many secret words as you can before time runs out! Challenge your friends and learn new words, one letter at a time!

Rules

The rules of Hangman are simple. Guess the word by selecting letters from the alphabet.

If you guess the word correctly, you win! If you guess the word incorrectly, you lose a life.

You have 10 lives. If you lose all your lives, you lose the game.

You can also use the keyboard to play. Press the letter on your keyboard to select it. Press the space bar to submit your guess.
