Rules

The rules of Hangman are simple. Guess the word by selecting letters from the alphabet.

If you guess the word correctly, you win! If you guess the word incorrectly, you lose a life.

You have 10 lives. If you lose all your lives, you lose the game.

You can also use the keyboard to play.

Press the letter on your keyboard to select it. Press the space bar to submit your guess.

Modern Hangman Web

Modern Hangman Web is a web version of the classic game Hangman.

It is made with HTML, CSS, Go and JavaScript for the front.
